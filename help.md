### Reporting a bug? Here is how to find bug reports and logs

1.  Open Windows Explorer.

2.  In the path entry box, paste ```%AppData%/OpenInWSL/```and hit enter.

3.  The logs are stored in ```main.log```.

4.  Include these logs in support emails or share them in the [Discord help server](https://discord.gg/VkvNgkH). Ensure no personal information is contained in them before sharing.
